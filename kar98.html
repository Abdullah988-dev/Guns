Detailed Description

The Kar98k is a legendary **bolt-action sniper rifle** known for its **one-shot kill potential** when landing headshots. It delivers extremely high damage and is ideal for **long-range combat**. Due to its bolt-action mechanism, each shot must be carefully aimed, rewarding skilled and patient players.

Weapon Stats in Detail

  • Damage per bullet: 75 (headshot: 250+)
  • Fire Rate: Very slow (bolt-action)
  • Reload Time: 4.0 seconds (full reload)
  • Effective Range: 100–800 meters
  • Recoil Pattern: Extremely high
  • Attachments Compatibility: 4x / 6x / 8x Scope, Suppressor, Cheek Pad
  • Ammo Type: 7.62 mm (high damage rounds)

Tips for Use

  • Always aim for headshots to maximize damage.
  • Use higher scopes (6x or 8x) for long-range engagements.
  • Best used from cover due to slow reload speed.
  • Pair with an assault rifle for close-range fights.

Comparison with Other Snipers

  • Higher damage than M24 but slower reload.
  • More skill-based weapon compared to DMRs.
  • Deadliest sniper for precise long-range players.
