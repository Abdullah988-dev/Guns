Detailed Description

The AWM (Arctic Warfare Magnum) is one of the most powerful bolt-action sniper rifles in the game. Known for its one-shot kill capability, the AWM delivers devastating damage even against armored enemies. Its long-range accuracy and high bullet velocity make it the top choice for elite snipers.

Weapon Stats in Detail

  • Damage per shot: 120+ (Headshot: Instant Kill)
  • Fire Rate: Very Slow
  • Reload Time: 4.6 seconds
  • Bullet Velocity: Extremely High
  • Penetration: Very High (Armor)
  • Ammo Type: .300 Magnum

Tips for Use

  • Always aim for headshots for instant kills.
  • Use 6x or 8x scope for long-range dominance.
  • Stay in cover while reloading.
  • Carry a close-range secondary weapon.

Comparison

  • More reliable than Kar98k.
  • Faster handling than AMR.
  • Best all-round sniper rifle for pro players.
