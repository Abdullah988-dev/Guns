AKM Assault Rifle

Powerful automatic rifle for mid-range combat

Overview

  • Type: Assault Rifle
  • Ammo: 7.62 mm
  • Fire Mode: Automatic / Single
  • Magazine Capacity: 30 rounds (standard)
  • Effective Range: 100–400 meters
  • Recoil: High
  • Damage: High
  • Attachments: Scope, Grip, Suppressor, Extended Mag

Detailed Description

The AKM is a modernized version of the AK-47 with enhanced durability and slightly reduced recoil. It is known for its **high damage per shot**, making it deadly at mid-range combat. Its 7.62 mm bullets deliver excellent penetration against enemies and light cover. Players often prefer it in open-field fights or mid-range engagements where its recoil can be controlled.

Weapon Stats in Detail

  • Damage per bullet: 49 (headshot: 120)
  • Fire Rate: 600 rounds/min
  • Reload Time: 2.2 seconds (magazine)
  • Effective Range: 100–400 meters
  • Recoil Pattern: High vertical + horizontal sway
  • Attachments Compatibility: Red Dot, 2x/4x Scope, Compensator, Extended Mag
  • Ammo Type: 7.62 mm, high penetration

Tips for Use

  • Use single fire mode for long-range accuracy.
  • Attach vertical grip to reduce recoil for continuous fire.
  • Pair with a 4x scope for mid-range precision shots.
  • AKM excels in mid-range, but avoid close-range fights if possible due to slower fire rate than SMGs.

Comparison with Other Rifles

  • More damage than M416 and M16A4, but slower fire rate.
  • Better penetration than SCAR-L, especially at long range.
  • High recoil requires skillful control.
