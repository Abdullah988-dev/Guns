Detailed Description

The M16A4 is a **semi-automatic assault rifle** with excellent accuracy and low recoil, making it perfect for **long-range engagements**. Its consistent damage and precision make it a favorite among skilled marksmen who prefer controlled shots over spray firing. The 5.56 mm ammo ensures low recoil and reliable performance.

Weapon Stats in Detail

  • Damage per bullet: 41 (headshot: 120)
  • Fire Rate: 700 rounds/min (semi-auto)
  • Reload Time: 2.3 seconds (magazine)
  • Effective Range: 100–400 meters
  • Recoil Pattern: Low vertical, very controllable
  • Attachments Compatibility: Red Dot, 2x/4x/8x Scope, Suppressor, Grip, Extended Mag, Compensator
  • Ammo Type: 5.56 mm, high accuracy

Tips for Use

  • Use semi-automatic mode for long-range accuracy.
  • Attach compensator and vertical grip for maximum control.
  • Excellent choice for players skilled in aiming precision shots.
  • Effective for picking off enemies from distance without exposing yourself too much.

Comparison with Other Rifles

  • More accurate than AKM and SCAR-L at long range.
  • Lower damage per bullet compared to AKM but easier to control.
  • Perfect for marksmen and long-range sniping in mid-range fights.
