Detailed Description

The SCAR-L is a **stable and versatile assault rifle**, ideal for players who want controlled recoil and reliable performance. Its moderate damage and fast fire rate make it suitable for **close and mid-range engagements**, while low recoil allows accurate follow-up shots. Compatible with multiple attachments, SCAR-L can be customized for different combat scenarios.

Weapon Stats in Detail

  • Damage per bullet: 43 (headshot: 112)
  • Fire Rate: 650 rounds/min
  • Reload Time: 2.4 seconds (magazine)
  • Effective Range: 100–300 meters
  • Recoil Pattern: Low vertical + slight horizontal sway
  • Attachments Compatibility: Red Dot, 2x/4x/8x Scope, Suppressor, Grip, Extended Mag, Compensator
  • Ammo Type: 5.56 mm

Tips for Use

  • Use automatic mode for close-range combat.
  • Attach compensator or vertical grip for better recoil control.
  • Ideal for mid-range fights due to accuracy and stability.
  • Good choice for beginners because of low recoil and easy handling.

Comparison with Other Rifles

  • Lower damage than AKM, but more stable and easier to control.
  • Faster fire rate than M16A4 for close-range fights.
  • Versatile weapon for both beginners and advanced players.
