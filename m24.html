Detailed Description

The M24 Sniper Rifle is a reliable bolt‑action precision weapon widely used by military and tactical units. It offers excellent accuracy and consistency at long distances, making it ideal for controlled sniper engagements.

Weapon Stats in Detail

  • Damage per shot: High (2 shots for kill, 1 headshot)
  • Fire Rate: Slow
  • Reload Time: 4.0 seconds
  • Bullet Velocity: High
  • Penetration: Medium‑High
  • Ammo Type: 7.62×51mm

Tips for Use

  • Use 6x or 8x scope for maximum accuracy.
  • Best for medium to long‑range combat.
  • Control breathing for accurate shots.
  • Pair with an AR or SMG for close combat.
