Detailed Description

The AMR (Anti-Material Rifle) is a powerful heavy sniper rifle built to eliminate targets at extreme distances. It is capable of dealing massive damage with a single shot and can penetrate armor, vehicles, and solid cover. Due to its heavy weight and slow fire rate, the AMR rewards patience and precision.

Weapon Stats in Detail

  • Damage per shot: 120+ (Headshot: Instant Kill)
  • Fire Rate: Very Slow
  • Reload Time: 4.5 – 6 seconds
  • Bullet Velocity: Extremely High
  • Penetration: Very High (armor & vehicles)
  • Ammo Type: .50 BMG / 12.7 mm

Tips for Use

  • Use AMR only for long-range engagements.
  • Always aim for headshots to maximize damage.
  • Stay behind cover due to slow reload speed.
  • Carry a secondary weapon for close combat.

Comparison

  • More powerful than Kar98k and M24.
  • Better penetration than AWM.
  • Harder to use due to heavy recoil and slow firing.
