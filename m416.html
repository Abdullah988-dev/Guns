Detailed Description

The M416 is one of the most versatile assault rifles in the game. It has **moderate recoil** and **high stability**, making it effective for both close and mid-range combat. Its fast fire rate allows players to deal consistent damage while maintaining accuracy. The 5.56 mm ammo ensures lower recoil compared to 7.62 mm rifles, giving it an edge in sustained firefights.

Weapon Stats in Detail

  • Damage per bullet: 41 (headshot: 115)
  • Fire Rate: 700 rounds/min
  • Reload Time: 2.3 seconds (magazine)
  • Effective Range: 100–300 meters
  • Recoil Pattern: Moderate vertical + low horizontal sway
  • Attachments Compatibility: Red Dot, 2x/4x/8x Scope, Suppressor, Grip, Extended Mag
  • Ammo Type: 5.56 mm, balanced penetration

Tips for Use

  • Use single fire or burst mode for long-range accuracy.
  • Attach vertical grip and compensator to minimize recoil.
  • Ideal for close to mid-range fights due to fast fire rate.
  • Combine with a 4x scope for mid-range engagements.

Comparison with Other Rifles

  • Lower damage than AKM but higher fire rate.
  • More stable than SCAR-L at mid-range.
  • Easy to control recoil, suitable for beginners.
